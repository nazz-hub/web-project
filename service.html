Ordering Instructions

To place an order:

  1. Select the items you wish to order from the menu.
  2. Click the "Order" button next to each item.
  3. For any special requests, please mention them in the comments section.
  4. Call the front desk or use the in-room tablet to confirm your order.
  5. Enjoy your meal!
